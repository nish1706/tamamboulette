Who’s Behind the Delicious Boulettes?

Our Story, Told Deliciously!

Once upon a time (well, 2019), in a cozy street corner in Mauritius, Tamam Boulettes was born! We started small – just a little stall with big dreams and even bigger flavors. Then the pandemic hit, and our little dream took a nap... but now, we’re back! And this time, we’re going digital!

Tamam is where tradition meets technology. We’re on a mission to keep the soul of Mauritian street food alive, while giving you a modern, finger-clicking experience. Because who says you can’t have heritage with a side of innovation?
